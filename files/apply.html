Apply to WashU Nursery School

If you are interested in Washington University Nursery School you should first contact the school to schedule a visit.

Then, complete the following application online or return the pdf version. You only need to complete this once:

WUNS Online Application Form

WUNS Application Form PDF

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school.

After the application and fee is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date.
